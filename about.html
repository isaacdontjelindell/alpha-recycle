About Us

Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.


We are a dedicated team of individuals willing to work with your business or organization, no matter what the project may be.

Alpha has a fleet of 5 trucks, over 140 dumpsters/containers, and 3 skid loaders allowing us to simplify our work loads.

In addition to new home debris removal and private home debris removal we also are licensed to haul clean and mixed demolition.

Our variety of different size containers/dumpsters offer flexibility when deciding on what size container is perfect for your job.

We have the ability to provide roll off containers for inner city alley drops. This saves on permit fees, & on street hassles due to snow plowing, snow emergency routes and street maintenance.

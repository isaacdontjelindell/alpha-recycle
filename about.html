About Us

Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.


We are a dedicated team of individuals willing to work with your business or organization, no matter what the project may be.

Alpha has a fleet of 5 trucks, over 160 dumpsters/containers, and 3 skid loaders allowing us to simplify our work loads.

In addition to new home debris removal and private home debris removal we also are licensed to haul clean and mixed demolition.

Our variety of different size containers/dumpsters offer flexibility when deciding on what size container is perfect for your job.

We have the ability to provide roll off containers for inner city alley drops. This saves on permit fees, & on street hassles due to snow plowing, snow emergency routes and street maintenance.

Resource Recovery

In August of 2005, Alpha Container Services & Recycling obtained a MPCA (Minnesota Pollution Control Agency) License to recover waste from construction sites. This allows us to sort through loads and combine items reusable and recyclable, and dispose of them at the proper facilities.


Total amount of waste recovered (tons) in 2012:

  • Wood - 273 tons or 601,863 lbs (wood waste to energy)
  • Metal - 102 tons or 224,872 lbs
  • Cardboard - 161 tons or 354,945 lbs

We have recycled Pallets for sale! (Please call 651-438-8855)

We have listed items we feel reusable on the MN Frugal Market.

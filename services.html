Roll-Off Dumpsters

Our containers/dumpsters are easy to load with easy access. We will provide a container that fits your specific job.


  • 10 cubic yard container/dumpster fits in a single car stall parking space and is approximately 16 feet long, 7 feet wide and 3 to 4 feet high.
  • 20 cubic yard container/dumpster fits in a single car stall parking space and is approximately 16 feet long, 8 feet wide and 5 to 6 feet high.
  • 30 cubic yard container/dumpster is the largest container available and is approximately 22 feet long, 8 feet wide and 5 to 6 feet high.

Contact us if you have questions or concerns when deciding on what size container is perfect for your job.

Contractor Services

Alpha provides debris removal services for new home construction, roofing, and remodeling projects, including:
  • 10, 20, & 30-yard containers
  • Finished square foot pricing
We are flexible, and are willing to accommodate contractors with specific requests. Contact us for rates and scheduling.

Public Services

Our services provide homeowners a choice of a 10, 20, & 30-yard container/dumpster when remodeling your home, tearing off an old roof or cleaning out your home. Contact our customer services rep for rates and scheduling.

Containers also available for cardboard and metal recycling for businesses interested in going green and being sustainable.

One-time Pickup Services

  • One time pick up services by appointment
  • 3 cubic yard minimum.
  • Applies to Dakota County residences only.
  • Contact us for rates and scheduling.

Materials Not Accepted

Alpha cannot accept the following materials:

  • Fluorescent Bulbs & Ballasts
  • Liquids
  • Hazardous Waste
  • Asbestos
  • Railroad Ties

Contractor Services

Alpha provides services for new home construction debris removal including; 10, 20, & 30-yard containers, finished square foot pricing with pen services, sheet-rock scrap loading and hauling services. We are flexible, and are willing to accommodate contractors with specific requests. Contact our customer services rep for prices, quotes and scheduling.

Public Services


Our services provide homeowners a choice of a 10, 20, & 30-yard container/dumpster when remodeling your home, tearing off an old roof or cleaning out your home. Contact our customer services rep for rates and scheduling.

Containers also available for cardboard and metal recycling for businesses interested in going green and being sustainable.

Roll-Off Services

Our containers/dumpsters are easy to load with easy access. We will provide a container that fits your specific job.

  • 10 cubic yard container/dumpster fits in a single car stall parking space and is approximately 16 feet 7 feet wide and 3 to 4 feet high.
  • 20 cubic yard container/dumpster fits in a single car stall parking space and is approximately 16 feet long 8 feet wide and 5 to 6 feet high.
  • 30 cubic yard container/dumpster is the largest container available, it is approximately 22 feet long 8 feet wide and 5 to 6 feet high.

One-time Pickup Services

One time pick up services by appointment. 3 yard minimum. Applies to Dakota County residences only. Contact our customer services rep for rates and scheduling.

Per-Item Disposal

The following items are charged per-item:

  • Refrigerators - $30.00 ea.
  • Commercial Appliances - $100.00 ea.
  • Washer, Dryer, T.V. Microwaves, Water Heaters, etc. - $30.00 ea.
  • Car & Truck Tires - $15.00 ea.
  • Furniture - $30.00 ea.
  • Electronics - $30.00 ea.
  • Mattresses or Box Springs - $30.00 ea.
  • Small Engines (Fluids Drained) - $25.00 ea.
  • Snow Blowers or Lawn Mowers - $30.00 ea.
  • Large Batteries, Car Batteries - $15.00 ea.

Materials Not Accepted

Alpha cannot accept the following materials:

  • Fluorescent Bulbs & Ballasts
  • Liquids
  • Hazardous Waste
  • Asbestos
  • Railroad Ties
